Parse -> CompiledWatchpoints

compiled watchpoints has variable area w/ 4 vecs:
bools, bytes, words, and nums (usize)

Each input referenced in logic is assigned index into a vec (which vec 
depends on type of input). The value is updated whenever it is updated in
the emulator. When an input is changed, a flag "evaluate_watchpoints" is set,
and the watchpoints will be re-evaluated at the end of this emulation cycle.

The actual logic will be a vec of FastOps, which will have indices to their
inputs and an index for their output. There will be special FastOps for LOG
and BREAK statements, which will do their respective tasks.
